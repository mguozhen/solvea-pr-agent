**Solvea Launches AI Receptionist Platform for Small Businesses — No Monthly Subscription Required**

*The first AI customer service platform that handles calls, live chat, email, and SMS simultaneously — starting at $0.05 per minute, with no monthly fee*

**SAN FRANCISCO, CA — April 3, 2026** — Solvea today announced the launch of its AI receptionist platform purpose-built for small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs). Unlike traditional AI customer service solutions that require expensive monthly contracts, Solvea operates on a usage-based model — businesses pay only for conversations handled, starting at $0.05 per minute, with nothing owed when the line is quiet.

**The Problem Solvea Solves**

For dental offices, law firms, HVAC contractors, plumbers, roofers, electricians, real estate agencies, and other service businesses, a missed call is a missed customer. When an owner is on a job site, in a client meeting, or simply after hours, inbound calls go unanswered — and most callers move on to the next result. Solvea's AI receptionist answers every call, qualifies leads, books appointments, and escalates to a human only when it truly matters.

**What Makes Solvea Different**

- **Omnichannel in one agent**: Handles phone calls, live chat, email, and SMS simultaneously — no separate tools required
- **No monthly subscription**: Pay only for active usage — starting at $0.05/min — with zero idle cost
- **Fully customizable**: Businesses can tailor the AI's knowledge base, tone, and workflows for their specific industry — no coding required
- **24/7 availability**: Never misses a call, after-hours inquiry, or weekend booking request
- **Instant setup**: Get a dedicated business phone number and go live in under 10 minutes

**Built for the Businesses That Keep America Running**

Solvea is designed for the service businesses that power local economies — the plumber who can't answer the phone mid-job, the dentist who's with a patient, the HVAC technician on a rooftop, the electrician in a crawl space. These owners lose real revenue every time a call goes to voicemail. Solvea ensures they never have to choose between doing the work and answering the phone.
